The Cursed Temple Awaits

Blinded by greed, many have sought, yet none have returned with what they sought.
Where three gods’ feet rest in stone, a cursed temple stands, long overthrown.
Riches once flowed, now turned to dust, yet those who seek may earn their trust.
Follow the echoes of forgotten chants, where divine grace and fate still dance.
